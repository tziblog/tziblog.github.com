The Black Dog - Music For Real Airports

"Music for real airports" is partly a reply to Brian Eno's work of the (almost) same name. It disorientate us with the first atmospheric ambient tracks only to lure us in compositions, many times rhythmic, strong with emotion sometimes positive and sometimes dark. Each track has a certain place in the wholeness of this concept album. Here utilitarian accompaniment is rather misleading.

Ocoeur - Light As A Feather

One ride taking different routes. Compositions that crosses techno, electro-acoustic, down-tempo and impose the IDM veil over the album also manage to deviate from it. The dreamy and optimistic mood that "Light as a feather" entails , falls back only for some instances of melancholy and dubsty anxious determination.

Soul Oddity - Tone Capsule

And if there is one out there we shouldn't care less because we choose to stand with the strange.
