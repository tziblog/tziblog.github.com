Flaque ‎– Mindscapes

Flaque makes his distinction by building beautiful soundscapes, delayed and airy sequenced melodies, and by manipulating kattoo-styled beats and rythms. “Mindscapes” in addition to the above, is an album full of sceneries, placing you sometimes passive in front of the movement of the landscape and sometimes active exploring eagerly your surroundings.

Loess ‎– Burrows

The sound of Loess, despite its dim similarity with Boards of Canada, secures its unique space in the IDM sphere and defines their musical approach. Ambient and downtempo with dystopian sequences, broken melodies and glitch hip hop beats, could vaguely describe what should be expected from this release.

r.roo - mgnovenie

In mgnovenie, some tracks flow with the ambient stream, others are delivered with a more orchestral structure and most of them are dressed with an emotional and melancholic mood. But r.roo has got his hands dirty with rhythm beats from classic idm-style to techno and dubstie.
