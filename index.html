Von Magnet - De L'Aimant

Von Magnet describe their music as electroflamenco.Mimetic's, the beat-mechanic of the group at that time,constructions familiarize with ritual, tribal and industrial music while the guitars bring the flamenco side of the album on our headphones delivering a plethora of rhythms. Von's and Magnet's voices are strong and theatrical. A new year's eve addition.

Mlada Fronta - Fe2O3

"Fe2O3" is about minimal evolving soundscapes and melodies accompanied usually with rhythmic noise and great industrial dance mechanics. But when we dealing with Mlada Fronta and especially in this album we know that we don't have a demonstration of rhythm beat mechanics nor a praise to the "means" of his field. This artist puts a great effort into arranging his sounds and synthesis to unveil certain feelings and purposes on each track.
