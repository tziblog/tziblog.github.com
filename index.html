Undermathic - Return to Childhood

Undermathic seems to constructs his tracks over a repeated sample base, which is enriched gradually with melodies and glitches. The result, in our opinion, melodic, rhythmic – sampled, industrial/idm. Wow.

Scanone - Scenes

Strong and complete compositions are really hard to find in each of the tracks of an EP. “Scenes” is like a collection with the most distinctive tracks of a full length album. Electro – glitch is Scanone’s main approach, but idm-focused and dub(ier) tracks come along. This is a monotony repellent 12" vinyl.
