Plaid - Scintilli

This time with Plaid, we are going to focus on playful and abstract melodies and multiple synth arrangements rather than "strange" and "intelligent" beats. "Scintilli" comes in different approaches and moods, often optimistic and joyful but mostly sophisticated. It must be our mainstream-ier post so far. Well, we like the album.

Kattoo - Megrim

Beats, break beats and mechanic hip hop rhythms support an album filled with daring symphonic/ kinda classical compositions and eastern sounds all on a dark, cinematic and melancholic (yet sometimes melodramatic) motive. Megrim reaches the boundaries of a concept album. But don't underestimate Kattoo because he is a science on beat and rhythmic manipulation.

Von Magnet - De L' Aimant

Von Magnet describe their music as electroflamenco. Mimetic's constructions, the beat-mechanic of the group at that time, familiarize with ritual, tribal and industrial music while the guitars bring the flamenco side of the album on our headphones delivering a plethora of rhythms. Von's and Magnet's voices are strong and theatrical. A new year's eve addition.
