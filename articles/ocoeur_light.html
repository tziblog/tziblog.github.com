Ocoeur - Light As A Feather

n5MD, 2013
October 17th, 2013

Light as a feather and so we travel. One ride taking different routes. Compositions that crosses techno, electro-acoustic, down-tempo and impose the IDM veil over the album also manage to deviate from it. Ocoeur takes full advantage of the music-box and xylophone like keyboard sounds (hopefully real) to structure some delightful tracks. The dreamy and optimistic mood that "Light as a feather" entails , falls back only for some instances of melancholy and dubsty anxious determination.

Tο τρένο από το πλατύ, η αλλαγή από το ΙC, η διαδρομή πάνω από τους γκρεμούς και ο αγκώνας κολλημένος στο παράθυρο. Τα μάτια έξω. Η μεγάλη διαφορά με τον υπόγειο σιδηρόταφο. Εκεί κοιτάς τον εαυτό σου στην αντανάκλαση του τζαμιού, αποφεύγεις βλέμματα και πολλές φορές ψάχνεις το κενό. Εδώ όμως γυρνάμε πίσω (σ)τον τροχό. Γραμμή Πλατέος - Φλώρινας/Κοζάνης.

There was a train where you could lower the window and have a smoke. An old man across you was sleeping. Hitting play on your Walkman you could deny the loud rail noise and watch outside the kids playing as you go away...on that train.

If you head for a fast trip then skip to "Resonance", "Dream Pursuit", "My Love", "Astral Projection", "1.11", and bonus "Kids Playing"
