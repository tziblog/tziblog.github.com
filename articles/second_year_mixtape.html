To Ashes - mixtape

TZiblog & SOunds, 2015
Aug 30, 2015

Two years have passed since TziBlog & SOunds launch. These two years we tried to suggest releases of the abstract and experimental electronic scene. We targeted releases that had some impact to us from the far and near past. We didn’t and don’t have any intention to promote artists or labels. That’s why we are a kind of reluctant to suggest new releases.

This year was poor. We couldn’t follow on the 2014’s flow. One factor for this is our schedules having a hard time to adjust with the blog needs. Other factors are maybes. Maybe it is our mood. Or maybe it is the narrowness of our music references.

The only certain thing is that the future will be more likely poor as the year that passed us. But we will continue. Stay tuned to our social side (twitter, RSS, mailing list) because occasionally revisiting the site won’t help you very much.

Μέσα από τις στάχτες του παλιού ξεπηδάνε πολλές καινούριες μικρές στιγμές. Για το νέο παλιό.

Now for this year mixtape we have again artists that we suggested on Tziblog but with tracks from different releases and some other artists that for certain reasons we couldn’t suggest this year.

Enjoy.
