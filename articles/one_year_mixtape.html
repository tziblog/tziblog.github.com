Night is our time - mixtape

TZiblog & SOunds, 2014
Aug 21, 2014

One year has passed since TZiblog & SOunds started to suggest releases of the abstract and experimental electronic scene. Since August of 2013, we suggested 25 releases from various artists.

For the 1st year's anniversary, we present a mix-tape including many of the artists that we covered in our articles. A mix of tracks that were not featured on our blog, from releases different to the ones suggested.

Για άλλη μια φορά ακόλουθοι της μόδας. Με μουσική για τις διακοπές. Για αυτούς που ξεκινάνε τώρα...

Enjoy.
